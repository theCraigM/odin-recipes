Damn Good Hot Wings

Don't be flightless on gameday next Sunday - get yer wings here!

Ingredients Needed:

  • full chicken - live
  • sauce
  • ranch
  • palette cleansing veggies
  • an empty tummy

Steps:

  1. Kill the chicken
  2. grill them wings and douse in sauce. Dip in ranch.
  3. consume.
  4. cleanse mouth with veggies.
  5. repeat ad infinitum because you're going to need a lot of chickens for a decent amount of wings, man.
